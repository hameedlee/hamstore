Rosehip Radiance Cream

4.5 (24 reviews) Write a review

A luxurious facial cream infused with organic rosehip oil to restore and rejuvenate your skin. This powerful formula combines natural antioxidants and vitamins to reduce fine lines, improve skin texture, and reveal a radiant complexion.

Key Benefits

  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Improves skin elasticity and firmness
  • Brightens and evens skin tone
  • Deeply hydrates and nourishes

Our Rosehip Radiance Cream is a luxurious facial treatment designed to transform your skin. Formulated with the finest organic rosehip oil, rich in vitamins A and C, this cream works to restore your skin's natural radiance while reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

The lightweight yet deeply nourishing formula absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue, making it perfect for both day and night use. Regular use helps to improve skin texture, boost collagen production, and protect against environmental damage.

Suitable for all skin types, especially mature or dry skin seeking rejuvenation and hydration.

Full Ingredients List:

Rosa Canina (Rosehip) Seed Oil*, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter*, Cetearyl Alcohol, Glycerin, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Ascorbyl Palmitate (Vitamin C), Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil*, Rosa Damascena Flower Oil*, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Oil*, Linalool**, Citronellol**, Geraniol**, Limonene**

*Organically grown ingredient
**Naturally occurring component of essential oils

Application Instructions:

  1. Cleanse your face thoroughly and pat dry
  2. Apply a small amount of cream to your fingertips
  3. Gently massage onto face and neck using upward circular motions
  4. Allow to absorb fully before applying makeup
  5. Use twice daily - morning and evening

For best results, use after our Rosehip Radiance Serum and follow with SPF during the day.
