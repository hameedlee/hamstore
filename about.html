Our Story

Crafting luxury experiences through artisanal creams and bespoke fashion designs since 2015

The Beginning

Hams Store was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create exceptional products that celebrate individuality and craftsmanship. Founded in 2015 by designer Hams Anderson, our boutique began as a small studio in New York's fashion district, where we handcrafted our first batch of artisanal creams and designed our initial custom pieces.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a destination for discerning customers who appreciated quality, attention to detail, and the luxury of personalized service. Our commitment to using only the finest natural ingredients and sustainable practices set us apart from the beginning.

The Art of Craftsmanship

At Hams Store, craftsmanship is at the heart of everything we do. Our creams are handcrafted in small batches to ensure maximum freshness and potency, while our fashion pieces are meticulously constructed by skilled artisans who share our passion for perfection.

We believe that true luxury is not just about premium materials, but about the care and attention that goes into every detail. From the hand-picked botanicals in our skincare to the hand-stitched seams in our garments, every element is thoughtfully considered.
